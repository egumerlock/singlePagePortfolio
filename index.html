About Me

Eric Gumerlock

I am a purposeful and passionate software developer who loves to create apps that are useful to people in their everyday lives, have practical and real-world application, and that make people’s lives simpler and easier. I’ve switched from the Environmental Science field to a brief stint in options trading, and finally to software development where I have found a lifetime of material to study, to be excited and animated by. I have always been an adept problem-solver, both logically and socially, and my team members have described me as a natural leader. My focus lies in front-end development for both mobile and web apps, and when I am not working on improving my efficacy with front-end frameworks, I am constantly thinking of new possibilities of incorporating applications into our daily lives.
